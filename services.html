Order types

J&J Tarts offers two different types of order types:

Stock Orders- These are selectable options of baked goods with varied options from large cakes to small donuts there are plenty of options for you to order for any occasion with love and care put into each of our baked goods at J&J tarts with most orders taking two to five days depending on order size.

Custom Orders- You can order online or in person with custom orders being for any event or occasion from wedding cakes to birthday cakes we offer a wide range of customisation for your orders (Note custom orders may take a long or short time to make depending on multiple factors including but not limeted to, large orders, staff shortages, excess orders. We apologise if we have inconvinienced you and we hope J&J Tarts can bake for you in the future.)


Pre-baked items

Our pre-baked items on offer in store at J&J Tarts are freshly baked in store and are perfect for quick bites to eat in store with a dining area being available to eat at as well as takeout options being available too. However pre-baked goods are limeted in size as larger items such as cakes are not available for purchase only for ordering.


Delivery

Our new delivery service allows you to enjoy J&J Tarts without even coming out the house with our iconic taste being available for items from cupckaes and doughnuts to cakes however larger cakes and other baked goods may not be deliverable as there is a risk that they may be damaged and at J&J Tarts we would not wish for any of our customers to get a sub par product and we apolgise for inconvinience. Our delivery times can vary depending on the item ordered and the date ordered however we can guarantee withen a week of ordering our products will be delivered.
